WE RISE
Working under the great direction of Robertino Zambrano and the very talented Mighty Nice crew,
we produced this animation for Commumity America featuring Kansas City superstar Patrick Mahomes.

This project was done using Adobe After Effects and Autodesk Maya. And, my role was to composite
all supplied 3D renders matching the style given by Robertino.
