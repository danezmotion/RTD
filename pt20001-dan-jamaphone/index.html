JAMAPHONE

The word "Jamaphone", is a made up word that means "calling phone".
"Llamar" in Spanish means "to call". And, When we remove the "R" from the word "Llamar", we get "Llama" which means "Someone calls".
The sound of the double "LL" is similar to the English sound for "J". So, if we simply replace the double "LL" to "J" we get "Jamaphone".

For this project I combined the research and development found in "Rays" and "Flow" to produce this promo for a fake brand of phones.
Side FX Houdini and Redshift were the main tools used during the full process.
